our clinic

outpatient services

The mission of the organization - a reliable guarantee of high quality health care services for each user. At the clinic "ENMEDIC" three branches: Central Clinic – outpatient inpatient clinic in Tbilisi and 2 branches (outpatient clinics) in Tbilisi and Batumi. Enmedic Clinic is a medical diagnostic facility equipped with modern technologies that empowers patients multidisciplinary outpatient and inpatient medical care and a full range of laboratory tests. The clinic operates branch liver disease, which offers conservative and surgical treatment of liver diseases (including intraoperative radiofrequency and microwave ablation of primary liver cancer and secondary metastatic his defeat). The clinic has a laboratory in which there is the possibility of up to 1000 types of research in the following diagnostics sections: biochemistry, hematology, immunology, bacteriology, morphology, genetics, serology, Allergology.
